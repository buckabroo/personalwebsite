Our focus is professional development for underclassmen studying technology

What Organizations Have I Participated In?

During my time at the University of Nebraska at Omaha, I was a member of the student ACM and ACM-W chapters. I was treasurer for ACM-W for a year, and got the group sponsorship from local companies. I volunteered as a teaching assistant for Mystery Code Society to help teen girls learn to make their own games. I attended several hackathons and the Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing in 2018.
