We support women in technology

Our focus is professional development for underclassmen studying technology

What Does the ACM-W Do?

We arrange speaking events for local companies, provide professional development workshops, and host networking opportunities. Members attend conferences like the Grace Hopper Celebration, and find mentors in local professionals.

What Do I Do?

As the Treasurer, it is my role to raise funds and manage the account. This includes outreach to local companies for support. This academic year, Union Pacific Railroad Foundation and Deliveron have granted funds for chapter activities.
